Troubleshooting Tips:

  • Check your browser: Use Chrome on Android or Safari on iOS for best AR support.
  • Allow camera permissions: AR requires access to your camera.
  • Good lighting: Ensure you're in a well-lit environment.
  • Stable surface: For marker-based AR, place the marker on a flat, non-reflective surface.
  • Update your browser: Make sure you're using the latest version of your browser.
  • Check internet connection: A stable connection is required to load 3D models.
